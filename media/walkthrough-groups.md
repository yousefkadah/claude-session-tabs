### Colored, named groups

```
▼ 🔵 Feature work            (3)     ← drag sessions in
▼ 🟠 Bugs                    (2)
▼ 🟢 Research                (1)
```

Create a group, pick a color, and **drag** conversations into it — just like
Chrome tab groups. Groups collapse and are remembered per workspace.

Right-click a group to rename, recolor, or delete it (sessions stay, they just
become ungrouped).
